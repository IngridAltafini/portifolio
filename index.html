About Me

My name is Ingrid, I'm 18 years old and I joined the IT area in 2022.

I got this opportunity through the Vem Ser Dev project, when the people involved made presentations at schools in my city, claiming to be a course on IT and English totally free, that there were many vacancies for this profession, that it was a unique opportunity and that we could not pass up. Me and other people from my school signed up and in about a week I knew I would be participating in the project. Since then I've put a lot of effort into the classes, managed to accomplish and learn everything I saw in the course, winning a prize for the best final project. I hope to do well from now on in my professional career, putting into practice everything I've learned and that I'm learning as the days go by. Let's code!
